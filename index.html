How To Have An Effective Mental Health Routine

Needed time per day: 2 Hours
Effectiveness Rating: 90% effective

Mental health is an extremeley important part of everyone's lives. Maintaining a good mental health is key to having a productive and enjoyable life. These simple steps will give you some everday tools that you can use in order to stay in good health.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Pen
  • Paper
  • Mirror
  • Healthy Food
  • Communication Skills

  1. Build Self-Esteem.

    Practice saying things you like about yourself in the Mirror. Question your inner-critic. Recognize and focus on your strengths.

  2. Cope With Stress.

    Take time throughout your day to meditate. Sitting in a quiet space and focusing on your breathing for 30 minutes per day can decrease stress, anxiety, and depression

    You aren't alone, remember that mental health is a global issue.

  3. Tend To Your Physical Health.

    Get at least 30 minutes outside per day. Elevating your heart rate everyday decreases stress levels. Getting 8 hours of sleep per day or more is also proven to decrease stress.

  4. Manage Negative Emotions.

    Put time aside every day for you to be by yourself so that you can reflect on your thoughts and feelings. Writing about them in a journal could be a good way to let them go.

  5. Maintain Healthy Relationships.

    Determine the value of your friendships often. Indicators of a healthy relationship often include trust, respect, listening, and freedom.
